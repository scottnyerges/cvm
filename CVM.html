Join us in Wine Country!

The CVM Symposium 2018: Exploring and Preserving Visual Music will explore the theories, histories and practices of Visual Music. It features two days of talks and presentations from international scholars, artists, students, curators and researchers, plus a final half day of special sessions. A series of screenings occur throughout, featuring historical and contemporary visual music works.

Set in Sonoma County's wine country, the symposium also features special events including an opening wine reception the evening of August 13. Presented in association with Sonoma State University.
